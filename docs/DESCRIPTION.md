# Unlock Your World: The Ultimate Interactive Travel Tracker

Every journey tells a story. Whether you’re a globetrotter crossing continents or a national park enthusiast exploring the great outdoors, Travel Tracker is the most interactive and comprehensive way to document your travels and visualize your explorer’s progress.

### TRACK WHERE YOU’VE BEEN
No more wondering exactly how much of the world you’ve seen! Get total numbers, and detailed percentages of your progress across diverse categories:
- **World Explorer**: Count and track every country you’ve visited across the globe.
- **US States**: Mark all 50 states as you road-trip from the Atlantic to the Pacific.
- **European Nations**: Discover and log your journey through the diverse countries of Europe.
- **Chinese Provinces**: Map out your travels across every province in China.
- **Indian States**: Track your progress through the vibrant and colorful states of India.
- **US National Parks**: A must-have for nature lovers! Track every national park you’ve experienced.

### INTERACTIVE VISUALIZATION
See your travels like never before! With high-resolution, interactive maps, you can zoom into specific regions and watch your map fill with color as you log new destinations. Get an instant snapshot of your "Travel Percentage"—how much of each category have you checked off your bucket list?

### CLOUD SYNC & BACKUP
Your travel memories are precious. Securely log in with Google or Apple ID to save all your data to the cloud. Whether you upgrade your phone or use multiple devices, your travel history is always with you and safely backed up.

### EXPORT & SHARE YOUR MAP
Your travel map is your trophy! Travel Tracker makes it easy to take your maps with you:
- **High-Res Downloads**: Save your personalized map as a beautiful image to your phone.
- **Instant Sharing**: Share your progress and percentages directly with friends and family on social media.
- **Data Export**: Export your travel data for your own records or to use with other applications.

### KEY FEATURES AT A GLANCE:
- **Diverse Category Tracking**: From world countries to US national parks.
- **Progress Percentages**: Know exactly how close you are to completing your travel goals.
- **Interactive UI**: A sleek, modern design that is easy and fun to use.
- **Cloud Secure**: Never lose your data with our secure login and cloud sync.
- **Optimized for Mobile**: Built for explorers on the move, with smooth performance and a responsive map.
